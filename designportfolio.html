Schwarzschild Metric Based Black Hole Simulation

Problem
A lack of understanding of general relativistic effects around a black hole, or an object whose Schwarzschild Radius is outside its physical body
Solution
Develop and design a numerical simulation to solve and display a particle affected by a black hole
Derive and understand Schwarzschild geodesics in order to create a set of differential equations to solve numerically
Implement an 4th order Runge-Kutta numerical solver to evaluate the system of equations
Display and render the particles using OpenGL rendering
